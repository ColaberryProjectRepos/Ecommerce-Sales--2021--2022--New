Problem Statement

During the years 2021 and 2022, e-commerce sales in the USA witnessed a remarkable surge, with a significant increase of 14.3% compared to total retail sales. Despite this substantial growth, factors such as intensified competition, supply chain disruptions, and evolving consumer preferences may have impacted sales figures. To ensure continued success, businesses must identify and address these challenges. By doing so, they can develop effective strategies to overcome obstacles, seize opportunities, and further drive the growth of e-commerce sales in the USA.
